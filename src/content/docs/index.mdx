## What this is

This is the complete, evidence-backed playbook for **Rambo** — distilled from a multi-round, adversarially-verified research program (refuted claims discarded, honest gaps flagged). It covers everything from the idea to implementation: the market, the validated pain the incumbents keep inflicting, the unfilled gaps, the full product and per-feature deep dives, the business model, and the go-to-market.

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="A real, validated wedge" icon="approve-check">
    The #1 recurring developer pain is **forced accounts & cloud lock-in** — and **secret leakage** (30,000+ public Postman workspaces caught leaking credentials). Rambo is local-first, your data never leaves your machine unless you choose to sync.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Agent-native" icon="rocket">
    A first-party **MCP server** + a **deterministic CLI** make Rambo the API client AI coding agents can *drive* — plus the first client with genuine **live LLM/agent-API debugging** (client-side only).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Lean & defensible" icon="setting">
    Closed-source, **near-zero fixed infra** (one sync backend), metered only on backend-synced resources, with a self-hosted enterprise tier that *reduces* our own compliance burden.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Honest, not hyped" icon="information">
    Market sizing is the realistic SAM (~$2.6B tools slice by 2030), not a TAM mirage. Every load-bearing claim is cited; unproven assumptions are labeled as open questions.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
